About StudentHub

StudentHub is a student-focused productivity platform designed to simplify academic life. Students often struggle to manage assignments, deadlines, schedules, and personal goals across multiple tools.

StudentHub brings these essential features together in one centralized platform, helping students stay organized and productive throughout their academic journey.
